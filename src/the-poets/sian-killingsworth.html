Siân Killingsworth’s poetry has appeared in Blue Earth Review, Typehouse Literary Journal, Stonecoast Review, Glass: A Journal of Poetry (Poets Resist), Mom Egg Review, and others. She has an MFA in poetry from the New School, where she served on the staff of Lit. She sits on the board of and is 2020-21 Anthology Editor for the Marin Poetry Center. You can read more about her at www.sianessa.com.
