Marti Stephen is a poet, trail runner, and mountain biker, in that order, who lives in Boise, Idaho. She treated herself to an MFA in poetry at the University of San Francisco in California. Her work has been published in The Denver Quarterly, Volt, and Greensboro Review, among other journals. She shares her life with a gentle Great Dane and a charming African Grey Parrot.
