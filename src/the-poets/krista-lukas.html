Krista Lukas has read her work and led workshops in the United States and Europe. Her essays, short stories, and interviews have been published in The Sun, Jewish Women's Literary Annual, and Los Angeles Review of Books. She is the author of a poetry collection, Fans of My Unconscious, poems from which appear in The Best American Poetry 2006 and The Writer's Almanac.
