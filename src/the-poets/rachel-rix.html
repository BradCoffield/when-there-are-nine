Rachel Rix received her MFA in Creative writing from Sierra Nevada University. Her poems have been published in Nebo, The Ravens Perch, and forthcoming in WLA. She was shortlisted for the Billy Collins Fish Anthology 2020 poetry contest. Rachel currently lives in Sacramento, CA where she is a CMT. She lives with her husband Adam and their two cats Floppy and Leo.
