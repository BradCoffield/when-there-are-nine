Luke Johnson lives on the California Coast with his wife and three kids. His manuscript in progress was recently named a finalist for the Jake Adam York Prize, the Levis through Four Way Press, the Vassar Miller Award and the Pollock through the University of Wisconsin. His poems can be found in Kenyon Review, Florida Review, Narrative Magazine, Frontier, Cortland Review, Thrush and elsewhere.
