Luke Johnson lives on the California coast with his wife and three kids. His poems can be found at the Kenyon Review, Narrative Magazine, Florida Review, Frontier, Thrush and elsewhere. His manuscript in progress was recently named a finalist for the Jake Adam York Prize with Milkweed Editions, The Levis through Four Way Press, The Vassar Miller Award and is forthcoming Fall of 2023 from Texas Review Press. website: lukethepoet.comÂ
