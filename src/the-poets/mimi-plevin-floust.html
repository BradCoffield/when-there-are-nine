Mimi Plevin-Foust is the winner of the Gordon Square Review Inaugural Poetry Contest. Her poems and articles can be found in Carve Magazine, Fourteen Hills, LearnVest/Forbes.com, POZ Magazine, Two Cities Review and more. To deliver fresh encounters with poetry, Mimi created Poetry Rx which offers poems in prescription vials as medicine for the soul [WARNING: MAY CAUSE FEELINGS OF JOY]. Plevin-Foust shares her 107-year-old home with assorted Airbnb guests as well as her husband, daughter, and cats. Instagram: @mimiplevinfoust Website: mimiplevinfoust.com/cleveland-poet/
